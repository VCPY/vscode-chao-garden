# Chao Actions and Behaviors

This document is a list of all the actions a chao can do.

## Idle State
Chao are mostly idle. If you hover your mouse over an idle chao, it will display a happy animation.

![Idle Chao](media/neutral_chao/neutral_chao_idle.gif)
![Happy Standing Chao](media/neutral_chao/neutral_chao_standing_happy.gif)

## Walking
Chao can walk left or right across the screen. They will continue moving until they reach the edge of the screen or decide to do something else.

![Walking Chao](media/neutral_chao/neutral_chao_walking.gif)

## Flying
Instead of walking, chao can sometimes choose to fly across the screen.

![Flying Chao](media/neutral_chao/neutral_chao_flying.gif)

## Falling
Chao are a little bit clumsy. Occasionally while moving, a chao might trip and fall.

![Falling Chao](media/neutral_chao/neutral_chao_falling.gif)

## Sitting
When idle, a chao has a chance to sit down and rest. If you hover over a sitting chao, it will display a happy animation.

![Sitting Chao](media/neutral_chao/neutral_chao_sitting.gif)
![Happy Sitting Chao](media/neutral_chao/neutral_chao_sitting_happy.gif)

## Looking at Code
![Chao Watching Code Right](media/neutral_chao/neutral_chao_code_watch_to_right.gif)

When a curious chao is close to your code editor, it might turn and have a look at your code. 
(Note: this also works if your explorer tree is aligned to the right)
